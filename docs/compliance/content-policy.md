# Content Policy and Enforcement Baseline

## Policy Classes

- `SAFE`: visible by default.
- `REVIEW`: limited visibility pending moderation.
- `HIDDEN`: removed from ranking surfaces.
- `SHADOWBANNED`: hidden from general discovery while preserving evidence.

## Enforcement Rules

- High unresolved report volume auto-hides and can auto-shadowban.
- Severe report reasons accelerate shadowban thresholding.
- Admin moderation actions must be reversible and auditable.

## Operator Procedure

1. Validate report reason quality and reporter uniqueness.
2. Apply least-privilege action needed for immediate risk reduction.
3. Record action rationale and follow-up in admin audit trail.
4. Re-evaluate after resolution evidence is available.
