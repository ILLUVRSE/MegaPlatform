# Freethrow Frenzy

## Controls (Touch + Mouse)
- `Arc Swipe` (primary): swipe toward the hoop to shoot. Direction sets aim and swipe speed/length sets power.
- `Hold & Release` (secondary): hold to charge power, drag left/right while holding to adjust aim, then release.
- `Swipe Sensitivity`: low / medium / high.

## Timing Meter and Assist
- `Timing Meter` on: release timing is graded by a moving meter. Center hits improve shot quality.
- `Timing Meter` off: shot quality depends only on aim and power.
- Timing window scales by shot distance and pressure.
- `Assist` slightly widens timing forgiveness and reduces drift.

## Modes
- `Timed 60s` (default)
  - Score as many points as possible in 60 seconds.
  - Streak multiplier scales from `x1` to `x3`.
  - Misses reset streak/multiplier.
- `3-Point Contest`
  - 5 rack spots around the arc.
  - 5 balls per rack (25 total).
  - Money ball policy: last ball in each rack is worth `2` points.
  - Final screen shows score, accuracy, and best streak.
- `HORSE` (vs AI or local hotseat)
  - Shooter selects a challenge spot and attempts first.
  - If made, opponent must match from the same spot or gains a letter.
  - First player to spell `HORSE` loses.

## Pressure Toggle
- `Pressure` on: pressure increases with streak/progression/difficulty.
- Pressure reduces timing window and raises miss variance.
- `Pressure` off: stable shot forgiveness for more casual play.
