# Penalty Kick Showdown

Penalty Kick Showdown is a soccer shooter focused on read-and-react goalie battles.

## Controls

- `Swipe Shot` (default): swipe from the ball toward the goal. Direction sets target, swipe speed/length sets power.
- `Tap Target`: tap a reticle inside the goal, set power on the meter, then press `Shoot`.
- `Sensitivity`: `low`, `medium`, `high` adjust shot responsiveness and variance.
- `Spin`: optional. In swipe mode, curved swipes add bend. In tap mode, use the spin slider.

## Modes

- `Classic 5`: five penalties, total goals and points decide score.
- `Sudden Death`: continue while scoring; first miss/save ends the run.
- `Pressure Ladder`: 10 rounds with escalating keeper difficulty and round bonuses.
- `Practice` (optional): unlimited kicks for target training.

## Scoring

- Goal: base points.
- Corner goal: bonus points.
- Perfect shot: bonus when aim and power land in tight tolerance.
- Ladder rounds add extra goal points as rounds advance.

## Difficulty Behavior

- `Easy`: slower keeper reaction, occasional wrong-direction dives.
- `Medium`: balanced reaction and read quality.
- `Hard`: faster dive and better read, still limited by realistic reach.

## Options

- `Assist`: reduces aim variance and improves forgiveness.
- `Spin`: enable/disable bending shots.
- `Controls`: swap between swipe and tap target schemes at match start.
