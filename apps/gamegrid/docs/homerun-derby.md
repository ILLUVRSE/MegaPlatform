# Homerun Derby

## Controls
- Tap/click to swing.
- Before pitch release, drag left/right to set aim lane:
  - Pull
  - Center
  - Oppo
- Aim is locked when the pitcher releases the ball.
- Timing Assist can widen perfect timing and soften near-miss penalties.

## Pitch Types and Tells
- Fastball: fastest straight path, bright seam flash tell.
- Curveball: visible bend and looping hand-motion tell.
- Slider: later break with red trail tell.

Pitch difficulty changes:
- speed
- break amount
- pitch mix frequencies
- time between pitches and windup pace

## Modes
- Classic (10 pitches): exactly 10 pitches.
- Timed (60s): continuous pitches for one minute.
- Duel vs AI: player 10 pitches, then AI 10 pitches.

## Scoring and Streaks
- Home runs score base points plus distance bonus.
- Consecutive HRs increase streak multiplier up to x3.
- Non-HR resets streak multiplier to x1.

Tracked match stats:
- score
- HR count
- best distance
- perfect hits
- strikeouts

## Difficulty Effects
- Easy: slower pitches, less break, softer mix.
- Medium: balanced speed, break, and pitch variety.
- Hard: faster pitches, stronger break, tighter timing.

In Duel mode, AI also scales with selected difficulty.
